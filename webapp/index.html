FAQ

Q: Is my identity actually protected, or is this a marketing twist?

A: You're safe here, we don’t ask for emails, locations, names, or phone numbers, no tracking is happening

Q: What if my account gets hacked?

A: No problem, none of your private information was collected or stored in our servers, hackers will get nothing.

Q: Will I get bullied? how am I protected?

A: Trolls are everywhere on FB, Twitter..etc and DUSK is no different, but we've taken steps to insure your safety and protect you, like community moderators, reporting users, muting people, and blocking people, but again no one really knows who you are, so how do you bully a faceless person?

Q: Will I be subject to Porn, Drugs or Harsh Violence on DUSK?

A: Not here, unlike Periscope, and FB live all videos are pixelated, so you will not be able to see porn, drugs or violence, rest easy, we got you covered.

Q: What can I talk about on DUSK?

A: You can talk about anything that might make you uncomfortable if your real identity was attached, like depression, politics, confessions, relationships..etc the world is your oyster on DUSK. If you’re consistantly offensive, everyone will block you and no one will ever see your broadcasts.
